To The One Who Lives Up to Her Name
An Ode To Precious
By Chevy Ostheldane Artiaga Mabini

Precious, your name whispers to my soul,
A melody soft, yet making me whole.
In your eyes, I see the stars align,
A majestic beauty, eternally divine.

Each word you speak, a gentle art,
Etching your name upon my heart.
You’re the poem I’ve longed to write,
My quiet muse, my guiding light.

Your smile, a dawn that breaks the night,
A fleeting glimpse, yet blanding right.
In every glance, a world unfolds,
A treasure more than words can hold.

Your presence feels like heaven’s glow,
A sacred warmth I’ll always know.
For you, my prayers rise high each day,
A love that time can’t take away.

Your beauty shines, so pure, so rare,
A masterpiece beyond compare.
Like faith that keeps my spirit strong,
You’re in my heart, where you belong.

Through trials, I’d walk a thousand miles,
Just to be blessed by one of your smiles.
A love devout, my soul confesses,
To you, my heart forever presses.
Forever enchanted by you
